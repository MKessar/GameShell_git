Mission goal
============

Before merging the improvement of Jilano with the usage of a new rune, the wizard of the castle gives you the advice to switch the pineapple ingredient to something more powerful: goat hair !

You decided to implement this modification striaght away before forgetting the advice. Look at the arcane book somewhere in the main tower to learn how to modify the spell. Then create a branch named power, checkout it, implement the modification and create a commit.
